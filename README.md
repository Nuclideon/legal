# Nuclideon Legal Documents Archive

> Public, non‑code repository for the canonical text of Nuclideon’s legal documents and their history.

A version‑controlled archive of Nuclideon’s **public** legal documents (e.g. Privacy Policy).

This repository provides full transparency and traceability of changes over time for compliance, auditability, and public reference.

## Contribution Guidelines

- **Who can contribute**: Nuclideon’s Legal, Privacy, Security, and designated Product representatives.
- **Public contributions**: External PRs are not accepted for legal text, but reporting issues, typos or broken links are welcome.
- **Style**: Plain, accessible English; avoid legalese where possible. Use Markdown and keep sentences short.
- **Accessibility**: Use headings, lists, and descriptive link text.

## Security & Privacy

- This repository contains public legal text only.
- No PII or customer data should be committed.
- Use private channels for privileged legal discussions and negotiations.
